About Me

I’m Menna Nasser, a passionate UX/UI Designer focused on delivering intuitive and engaging digital experiences. My design philosophy centers on usability, creativity, and consistency. I specialize in using Figma to build complete design systems, wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity interfaces.

I’ve worked on a variety of projects, from mobile apps to responsive websites, always keeping the user at the heart of every decision. I pay close attention to interaction design, user flows, and accessibility to ensure exceptional results.

Tools I use: Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, Trello, Notion, and Miro.
